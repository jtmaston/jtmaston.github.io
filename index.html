Who am I?

   First and foremost, hello! Welcome to this beautiful madness that is my blog. Here, I ( sometimes ) post about my projects, fun little activities. That out of the way, who am I? I'm
( canonically ) a steam-powered penguin dwarf, who loves tinkering, building things that have a tendency to explode or otherwise catastrophically fail.


On the side, I also write stuff. Mostly poetry, but sometimes also prose. Most of it is in Romanian ( which is my native language ) but there are peices that are in English. You'll find both of these linked in the top bar, under the "Creative Writing" section.

My projects

Here's a helpful list of ongoing, completed and on hold projects that I've done / been doing in the past. I've also included some future plans for things that I'm ( hopefully ) going to learn.


Had my first run-in with Assembly, working on an AARCH64 calculator. It was fun! You can find the project here

My next project still involves Assembly, but this time I'll be targetting PowerPC, to get a bit into that retro action. More updates will follow as the project progresses.

I'm always looking forward to learning new things! Both concerning code and other stuff, I'm open to new challenges!
